King Bros Boat Storage

King Bros. Boat Storage is a family owned and operated company that has been in business since 1984. Being a family of very active boaters, the Kings understand the needs of all levels of boating enthusiasts, from first-time boat owners to seasoned pros. Whether you have a 42′ Fountain or a jet ski, King Bros. has a perfect accommodation for your craft!

The facility is enclosed by a wrought iron fence with rolling gates. The security features provide peace-of-mind for clients, since there is an onsite security guard. Customers have safe and convenient 24-hour access to their storage units using an individual key code.

We accept Visa, MasterCard, Discover and American Express! One month free service with annual pre-payment!
